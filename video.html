Pitter Patter Rain video song |

“Pitter Patter Raindrops” is a fun and gentle poem for little children. In this rhyme, tiny raindrops fall from the sky making a soft pitter-patter sound. The poem helps kids imagine rain dancing on the roof, leaves, and puddles while nature becomes fresh and green. This simple rhyme is perfect for preschool and kindergarten children to enjoy the beauty of rainy days while learning rhythm and new words. Through this poem, kids can listen to the sound of raindrops, imagine jumping in puddles, and enjoy the magic of rain.
